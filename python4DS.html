🚗 Automobile Market Analysis

This project was developed as part of the Python for Data Science module in my Postgraduate Program in Data Science. The objective was to analyze a dataset from an automobile company to uncover insights into customer demand and identify target demographics for various vehicle segments.

🔍 Project Objectives

  • Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to understand customer behavior and market trends.
  • Segment customers based on demographics such as age, income, and region.
  • Identify high-demand vehicle categories and underperforming segments.
  • Generate actionable insights to support marketing and product strategy.

📊 Tools & Techniques

  • Python
  • Pandas, NumPy for data manipulation
  • Matplotlib, Seaborn for data visualization
  • Jupyter Notebook for analysis and reporting

📈 Key Outcomes

  • Identified top-performing vehicle segments and their associated customer profiles.
  • Visualized regional and demographic trends influencing purchase decisions.
  • Provided strategic recommendations to align product offerings with market demand.
